About Me

Resume / CV

I’m an AI Research Engineer at Inventec, focusing on building reliable Computer Vision systems that can be deployed in the real world. My recent work includes an explainable defect inspection pipeline (detection → anomaly heatmaps → classification) for manufacturing, validated with cross-generation robustness (<1% FN, <20% FP), and a diffusion-based defect synthesis strategy that reduces real defect data needs by ~60%.

Interests: industrial anomaly detection, foundation models for vision, data-efficient learning, segmentation, and practical evaluation/verification workflows.
